Fantastic Beasts

Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them is a 2016 British fantasy film directed by David Yates and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. A spin-off prequel of the Harry Potter film series, the film was produced and written by J. K. Rowling (in her screenwriting debut), inspired by her book of the same name.

Newt Scamander

The entire premise of Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them centers around the character of Newt Scamander, a British employee of the Ministry of Magic and a "Magizoologist" who studies creatures —hence the trip to New York with a briefcase full of beasts.

Propentina Goldstein

Born in the USA in 1901, Propentina "Tina" Goldstein lost both of her parents at a very young age, learning to survive independently with her sister Queenie from the very start. Both attended Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
